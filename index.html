The First Law Trilogy

By Joe Abercrombie

Book 1 - The Blade Itself, Book 2 - Before They Are Hanged, Book 3 - Last Argument Of Kings

Series Summary

  • The First Law is a fantasy series written by British author Joe Abercrombie. It consists of a trilogy and three stand-alone novels set in the same world.
  • Written by author Joe Abercrombie.
  • The series follows six characters who's stories eventually begin to overlap and compete with each other.
  • The tone of the books can best be described as dark and gritty fantasy and could easily be recommended to fans of A Song of Ice And Fire/Game of Thrones.
  • The series has since been expanded with 3 standalone novels set in the same world and another triology of books that take place 15 years after the original triology.
  • My favourite character within the series is Logen Ninefingers given his character development and archetype as a barbarian hero within the story.

More information on the author Joe Abercrombie and his works can be found on his website.
